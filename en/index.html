ABOUT US

We are a young, entrepreneurial team based in Antigua Guatemala. We are passionate about partnering with good people and organizations to produce exceptional work that betters the world around us.

We understand that there is an incredible amount of people and businesses (for profit and not) doing great work by generating positive impact in society. Unfortunately, their work is not always known or recognized. Even more, small ones, startups, or CSR departments inside big companies don’t have the budget or people to make sure they showcase their work to the world.



We are here to fix that.

We are a team that is passionate about helping these businesses project their impact to the world. There are a lot of good actions being done, but not enough people know about them.
